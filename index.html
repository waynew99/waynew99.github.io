I am a 3rd year PhD candidate in CSE at the University of Michigan Ann Arbor, advised by Prof. Ang Chen. My research interests lie in the intersection of systems, networking, and security, with a current focus on applying systems approach to large infrastructures. Previously, I've worked on mobile security, network measurement, and congestion control. Beyond research, I care about teaching and mentoring students in computer science.

Before joining UMich in 2023, I received my B.A. in Computer Science from Middlebury College.
